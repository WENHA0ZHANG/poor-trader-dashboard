Market Regime Algorithm

Formula
score  =  Σi  tier(xi) × wi
xicurrent reading of indicator i
tier(xi)signed extremeness: ±1 mild, ±2 strong, ±3 extreme
wiquality weight (see table →)
score > 0bottom / buy bias
score < 0top / caution bias
